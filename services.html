Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at SMVITM started functioning in the year 2010. The Department offers an undergraduate program in mechanical engineering that covers a wide range of topics in the field of mechanical engineering such as thermal and fluid sciences, engineering design, dynamics and control, materials, manufacturing, modelling and simulation as per the VTU curriculum.
